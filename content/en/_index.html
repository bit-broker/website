Welcome to BitBroker

Share data with confidence

BitBroker is an end-to-end, open-source solution which allows organisations to share data within the context of robust policy. BitBroker can catalog complex, diverse, heterogenous datasets. Data owners can then create and deploy policies controlling: what can been seen, who can see it, how they can access it and the legal context for the exchange.
